Kendall's Baked Ziti Pasta

Description

A delicious baked ziti with both marinara and alfredo sauce.
This is my mom's recipe!
Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: | 30 minutes | Total Time: | 45 minutes | Calories: 531 kcal |

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ziti Pasta
  • 1 Jar of Rao's Marinara Sauce
  • 2 Cup of Kraft Italian Five Cheese Blend
  • 1/2 Cup of Kraft Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Cup of Kraft Mozzarella Cheese

    For Alfredo Sauce

  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup Kraft Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder *Or to taste*
  • Salt and pepper to taste

      Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees. Grease a 9 x 13 baking dish.
    2. Cook your pasta according to the package but leave it a little under-done, so it is not too soft. It will soften and cook a bit more in the oven. When the pasta is cooked just under-done, drain it, and rinse it in cold water so it stops cooking. Set it aside.

      For Alfredo Sauce

    1. Melt your butter in a skillet over medium heat and add flour and minced garlic. Whisk it together and let it cook for 1-2 minutes until the garlic becomes fragrant and the butter and flour bubble.
    2. Add your milk to the butter and flour and whisk it together until mixed well. Continue to whisk the sauce over medium heat until it starts to thicken.
    3. Add your milk, parmesan cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Then stir until the sauce is thick and smooth.

      Making the Pasta Dish

    1. Pour the pasta in a greased 9×13 baking dish.
    2. Mix the Alfredo and Marinara Sauce. You can adjust the seasonings at this point as well if you need to. You can add more garlic powder, salt etc.
    3. Pour Sauce on the cooked pasta. Mix well.
    4. Take half of your grated mozzarella cheese and mix in the pasta and sauce.
    5. Cover baking dish with foil.
    6. Bake at 375° for 30 minutes. Take the foil off the last 10
    Congratulations! You've just made the best Ziti Pasta bake to ever exist.

    Kwame's Ghanaian Meat Pies

    Description

    Flaky pastry filled with minced beef, onions and green peppers.
    These meat pies are delicious and a popular Ghanaian snack.
    Servings: 8 | Prep Time: 1 hour | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Calories: 4127kcal |

    Ingredients

      Meat Pie Filling
    • 1 tablespoon sunflower oil
    • 300 grams, minced beef
    • 1 medium onion, chopped
    • ½ teaspoon, all purpose seasoning
    • 1 small maggi stock cube
    • Salt to taste
    • 1 large green pepper, chopped into small cubes
      Pastry
    • 400 grams (3⅓ cups) self raising flour
    • 255 grams (1 cup plus 1 tablespoon) cold margarine or butter
    • Pinch of salt
    • 60 ml (¼ cup) cold water
    • 1 egg, whisked

    Instructions

      Minced Beef Filling
    1. Add a little oil to a large frying pan and heat up. Add the minced beef and onions and cook
      on medium heat until it browns.
    2. Mix in tomato puree and cook for 2 minutes. Add in all purpose seasoning stock cube and salt.
      Remove from heat and leave to cool, then stir in chopped green peppers.
      Pastry
    1. Sift flour and salt into a large mixing bowl.
    2. Add margarine to the flour and rub in using your fingertips. Add the cold water and mix.
    3. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured work surface and knead very lightly.
    4. Shape the dough into a disk and wrap tightly in plastic wrap and refrigerate until well chilled, at least an hour or overnight.
      To Assemble
    1. Pre-heat oven to 180°C fan/200°/400°F.
    2. Line a large baking tray with greaseproof paper.
    3. Lightly dusk a work surface and a rolling pin with flour. Roll out chilled dough.
      (This should be carried out in short, sharp stroked with light even pressure in a forward movement only. Turn the pastry as you roll.)
    4. Using either a saucer or a large circle cutter, cut circles in the dough.
      Place a quarter cup of filling in the centre of the circle.
    5. Fold dough over making it into a semicircle. Take a pastry brush and dip in water and moisten edges of dough circle then pinch sides together with a fork.
      Use a fork and poke holes on the top of the meat pies.
    6. Place meat pies on the baking tray.
    7. Brush the tops of pies with egg wash and bake in oven for 25 minutes or until the pies are golden brown.

    Aziz's Pancakes

    Description

    Delicious fluffy Pancakes that will have you wanting more!
    This is super easy to make!
    Servings: 6 | Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Calories: 1100kcal |

    Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 large egg

    Instructions

  • In a bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together.
  • Once stirred add milk, melted butter, and egg into bowl.
  • Heat pan over medium-high heat. Pour batter onto the pan using approximately 1/3 cup for each pancake.
  • Cook until bubbles form in about 2 to 3 minutes. Flip and cook until the other side is browned.
  • Repeat with remaining batter. Congratulations! You've just made the fluffiest most delicious Pancakes on the planet!

    Ken's Baked Steak & Potatoes

    Description

    This protein-packed meal is simple, quick, and affordable! Contents contain steak, potatoes, and shredded cheese guaranteed to be flavorful and fulfilling!
    Highly recommended for large-size families who need something quick to whip up. Also personally recommended for college students on a budget and those who aren't too fond of the kitchen.
    Yield: 4 Servings | Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 50 minutes

    Ingredients

    • 1 pound of Beef Stew Meat
    • 1 pound of Baby yellow potatoes
    • 8 ounce bag of shredded Mexican Cheese
    • 1/2 teaspoon of tony chacheres and Black Pepper
    • 3 tablespoons of butter
    • 1 teaspoon of parsley

    Intructions

    1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
    2. In a large pot bring potatoes to a rolling boil for approximately 15 to 20 minutes. While the potatoes are boiling, season beef stew meat with tony chacheres, black pepper, and any other seasonings of your liking.
    3. Remove potatoes from large pot and cut into small cubes. Then combine potatoes,beef stew meat, and butter into a deep dish baking tray.
    4. Cover baking tray with aluminum foil. Place baking tray and its contents into preheated oven for 40 minutes.
    5. Once the 40 minutes have passed, remove baking tray from oven and pull the foil back. Apply shredded cheese on top of the entire dish and reapply the aluminum foil.
    6. Place baking tray back into the oven and let cook for another 10 minutes.
    7. Remove from oven, garnish with parsley, and serve!

    Kori's Oreo Balls

    Description

    Oreo balls are a delicious and easy-to-make treat that combines the classic flavors of Oreos and cream cheese, coated in chocolate. They're perfect for parties, gatherings, or just as a sweet indulgence.

    Ingredients

    • 1 package (14.3 oz) Oreo cookies
    • 1 package (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
    • 1 package (12 oz) semi-sweet chocolate chips
    • Optional toppings: crushed nuts, sprinkles, shredded coconut

    Instructions

    1. Place the Oreo cookies in a food processor and pulse until they are finely crushed.
    2. In a mixing bowl, combine the crushed Oreos with softened cream cheese until well blended.
    3. Using your hands, roll the mixture into small balls, about 1 inch in diameter, and place them on a baking sheet lined with wax paper.
    4. Chill the Oreo balls in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour to firm up.
    5. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the chocolate chips in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until smooth.
    6. Dip each chilled Oreo ball into the melted chocolate, using a fork to coat evenly. Tap off any excess chocolate.
    7. Place the chocolate-coated Oreo balls back on the wax paper-lined baking sheet.
    8. Optional: Immediately sprinkle with your choice of toppings while the chocolate is still wet.
    9. Refrigerate the Oreo balls until the chocolate coating is set, about 30 minutes.
    10. Serve and enjoy!

    Synthia's French Toast

    Ingredients:

    • 4 slices of bread
    • 2 eggs
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • Butter or oil for frying
    • Maple syrup (optional)
    • Powdered sugar (optional)

    Instructions:

    1. In a shallow dish, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon.
    2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or oil.
    3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly.
    4. Place the coated bread slices in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.
    5. Remove from the skillet and serve hot with maple syrup and powdered sugar if desired.

    Leona’s Quick & Easy Chicken Alfredo Recipe

    Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Pasta
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Seasoning
  • Parmesan

    How to Make:

    Pasta

    1. Add ¾ of water to cooking pot – add a slice of butter – make sure stove is on medium high.
    2. Once bubbles start to form, add your noodles, and stir until the pasta become al dente.
    3. Once they are al dente, drain the water out the pot – and sit pasta to the side.

    Adding Sauce

    1. Add Alfredo Sauce into the pot full of pasta – stir well - keep stove top on low heat.
    2. Once the sauce is evenly distributed - add the parmesan cheese – stir well.
    3. Place lid on pot - sit pasta to the side.

    Chicken

    1. Prep your chicken before you cook it – this includes cleaning, removing fat, cutting, etc.
    2. Once the chicken is prepped – start seasoning until you start sneezing or until your ancestors tell you to STOP!Add any seasoning of preference but the basics to add is onion and garlic powder.
    3. Set stove to medium – add some oil to frying pan.
    4. Once the oil starts to sizzle, add a piece of chicken to the pan – let it sit for 3 minutes until the chicken starts to change brown. Flip chicken over repeat! If the chicken still has pink in the middle - let the chicken cook for a few more minutes.
    5. Repeat step 4 until all of the chicken is finished!

    Dinner is served.. Bon Appétit!!

    Baron's Burger Patty

    Description

    The best burger recipe that works very well with a grill! This juicy burger is packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments and sauces.

    Ingredients

    1. 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
    2. 1/2 onion
    3. 1/2 cup of shredded colby jack
    4. 1 egg
    5. 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
    6. 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
    7. Chick fil a sauce
    8. 1 teaspoon dried parsley
    9. 1 teaspoon dried basil
    10. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    11. 1/2 teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
    12. salt and pepper

    Recipe

    1. Preheat the grill for high heat and oil the grate
    2. Combine ground beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix, minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Use your hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.
    3. Cook patties on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 4 to 5 minutes per side.
    4. You then grab a burger bun and any other ingredients you prefer in your burger then enjoy your Baron Burger!
